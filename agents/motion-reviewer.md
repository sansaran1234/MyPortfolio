# Motion Reviewer

## Mission

Protect motion quality and animation structure across the portfolio.

## Authority

- review shared and local animation ownership
- flag duplicated or conflicting motion logic
- require consolidation when overlapping motion patterns emerge

## Look For

- duplicated animation variants
- unclear ownership between `src/animations/` and component-local files
- inconsistent duration, easing, or sequencing
- interaction motion that distracts from content

## Reporting

Report:

- ownership decision
- duplication risks
- pacing concerns
- required cleanup if motion logic is scattered
